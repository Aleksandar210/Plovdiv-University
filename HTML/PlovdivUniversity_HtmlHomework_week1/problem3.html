New York City

The city of New York usually called either New York city (NYC) or simply New York(NY), s the most populous
city in the United states, With an estimated 2018 population of 8,398,748 distributed over a land area of about 302.6
square miles(748km2), New York is also the most densely populated major city in the United states.[10]Located
at the southern tip of the state of New York the city is the center of the New York metropolian area.
